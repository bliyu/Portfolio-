Career Philosophy

I believe technology should be used to build solutions that are practical, reliable, and helpful in everyday life. My interest in software development comes from curiosity about how systems work and how thoughtful design and programming can solve real problems in a clear and organized way.

As a Computer Programming and Analysis student at George Brown College, I have learned that growth in this field requires continuous learning, patience, collaboration, and strong problem-solving skills. My goal is to become a well-rounded developer who can contribute to meaningful projects, work effectively with others, and continue adapting to new technologies as the field evolves.
